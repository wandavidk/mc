File Format Guide

Your text file should follow this format for each question:

[Question text]
A) [Choice A]
B) [Choice B]
C) [Choice C]
D) [Choice D]
Answer: [Correct choice letter]
Explanation: [Explanation text]

[Next question...]

Example:

What is the value of 3² + 4²?
A) 5
B) 7
C) 12
D) 25
Answer: D
Explanation: 3² is 9, 4² is 16, and 9 + 16 = 25.

What is 45% of 200?
A) 45
B) 90
C) 100
D) 180
Answer: B
Explanation: 45% of 200 is 0.45 × 200 = 90.
